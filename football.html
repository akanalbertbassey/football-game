Top Football Players

Discover the best players in football, their age, biography, salary, achievements, and images.

Lionel Messi

Age: 37

Salary: $75 million per year

Lionel Messi is widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time. He has won multiple Ballon d'Or awards and is known for his incredible dribbling, vision, and playmaking abilities.

Achievements:

  • 7x Ballon d'Or Winner
  • 4x UEFA Champions League Winner
  • 10x La Liga Winner
  • 1x FIFA World Cup Winner (2022)

Cristiano Ronaldo

Age: 39

Salary: $60 million per year

Cristiano Ronaldo is a legendary forward known for his athleticism, goal-scoring ability, and leadership on and off the pitch. He has won numerous titles across various leagues, including the Premier League, La Liga, and Serie A.

Achievements:

  • 5x Ballon d'Or Winner
  • 5x UEFA Champions League Winner
  • 7x League Titles (Premier League, La Liga, Serie A)
  • 1x European Championship Winner (2016)

Neymar Jr.

Age: 32

Salary: $70 million per year

Neymar is one of the most skillful players in the world. Known for his flair, creativity, and goal-scoring prowess, Neymar has made a significant impact at both the club and international level.

Achievements:

  • 1x Copa Libertadores Winner
  • 2x UEFA Champions League Winner
  • 1x Olympic Gold Medalist (2016)
  • 1x Copa América Winner (2013)

Kylian Mbappé

Age: 25

Salary: $110 million per year

Kylian Mbappé is known for his incredible pace, dribbling, and goal-scoring ability. He is considered one of the brightest young stars in world football, already winning multiple league titles and a World Cup.

Achievements:

  • 1x FIFA World Cup Winner (2018)
  • 5x Ligue 1 Winner
  • 1x UEFA Nations League Winner (2021)

Robert Lewandowski

Age: 36

Salary: $45 million per year

Robert Lewandowski is one of the best strikers in football history, known for his goal-scoring record and incredible physicality. He has been a dominant force in the Bundesliga for many years.

Achievements:

  • 1x FIFA Best Player Award
  • 8x Bundesliga Winner
  • 1x UEFA Champions League Winner

Mohamed Salah

Age: 32

Salary: $22 million per year

Mohamed Salah is known for his speed, dribbling, and exceptional goal-scoring ability. He has been a key player for Liverpool, leading them to multiple Premier League and Champions League titles.

Achievements:

  • 1x Premier League Winner
  • 1x UEFA Champions League Winner
  • 2x Africa Cup of Nations Winner

Virgil van Dijk

Age: 33

Salary: $15 million per year

Virgil van Dijk is widely considered one of the best defenders in the world. His leadership, aerial ability, and composure on the ball make him a crucial part of Liverpool's defense.

Achievements:

  • 1x UEFA Champions League Winner
  • 1x Premier League Winner
  • 2x FIFA Best Defender

Kevin De Bruyne

Age: 33

Salary: $20 million per year

Kevin De Bruyne is widely regarded as one of the best midfielders in the world, known for his passing range, vision, and ability to create goal-scoring opportunities.

Achievements:

  • 4x Premier League Winner
  • 1x UEFA Champions League Winner
  • 2x Premier League Player of the Season

Luka Modrić

Age: 38

Salary: $12 million per year

Luka Modrić is one of the best midfielders of his generation. Known for his vision, passing, and composure, he has been a key player for both Real Madrid and Croatia.

Achievements:

  • 1x Ballon d'Or Winner
  • 5x UEFA Champions League Winner
  • 1x FIFA World Cup Runner-up (2018)

Sergio Agüero

Age: 36

Salary: $10 million per year

Sergio Agüero is one of the best goal-scorers of his generation. Known for his positioning and finishing ability, he is a club legend at Manchester City.

Achievements:

  • 5x Premier League Winner
  • 1x FA Cup Winner
  • 1x Copa América Winner (2021)
